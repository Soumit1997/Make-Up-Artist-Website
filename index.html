About Surojit

Surojit is a freelance Makeup and Hair stylist with over 10 years of experience in the industry.
He trained at Jawed Habib Hair and Beauty Pvt. Ltd. for hair styling, and
attended training at Lakme Training Academy and Vidya Tikariz to become a certified Makeup artist. Surojit worked as instructor at Jawed Habib Hair and Beauty Pvt. Ltd. and as Beauty Manager and Makeup artist at Lakme Training Academy, Vidya Tikari and Home Shop 18 at Film City Noida for 10 years. Now he opened his own business as a freelance Hair/Makeup Artist.
